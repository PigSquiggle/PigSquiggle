RULES


Art Square:

Use the arrow keys to move the black square around. Click the black rectangle in the bottom left of the box to choose the square's color. Press space to make a stamp of the square. Press the + and - keys to change the square's size. Press shift + 1 to make the square taller and press option/alt + 1 to make the square shorter. Press shift + 2 to make the square wider and option/alt + 2 to make the square less wide. Click a stamp to make remove it.
