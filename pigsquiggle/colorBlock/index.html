Information

When you click anywhere, the block makes
a path to wherever you click.


The path slowly disappears but if
you dont want that to happen, click
on the Trail fade button or press
T to toggle Trail fade.


There is a glitch where if the block
is moving somewhere, and you
click somewhere else, the block will
do weird stuff and make duplicates.


You can turn the glitch off by clicking
the glitch button or clicking G to toggle glitch.


You can tell if the buttons/features
are enabled if they have a yellow border.


You can turn on the One color feature
by clicking the One color button or
clicking O to toggle One color so your square
doesn't change colors when it moves.


If you have the One color feature on,
you can press C to open the color
picker or click the black bar
under the One color button to choose the color
of the one color the block is. (If you are in the
color picker, you can press the Esc key to exit it)
